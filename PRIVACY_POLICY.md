# Privacy Policy

**Score247**  
Last updated: March 2026  
Maintained by: Waleed Ahmed (waleedahmedja)

---

## The Short Version

Score247 collects nothing. It stores nothing outside your device. It sends nothing anywhere. There are no accounts, no analytics, no ads, and no internet connection of any kind.

---

## What Data Score247 Collects

**None.**

Score247 does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no internet permission and cannot communicate with any server.

---

## What Data Score247 Stores

Score247 stores match state locally on your device using Android's DataStore — this includes:

- Team names you enter
- Player names you enter
- Match scores and ball-by-ball events
- Your custom rule selections

This data exists solely to let you resume a match after closing the app. It is stored in your device's private app storage. It is never shared, uploaded, or backed up to any external service.

Uninstalling Score247 permanently deletes all locally stored data.

---

## Permissions

Score247 requests **no permissions** from Android. It does not access your camera, microphone, contacts, location, storage, or any other system resource.

---

## Third-Party Services

Score247 uses **no third-party SDKs, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or crash reporting services** of any kind.

---

## Children

Score247 does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children. Since the app collects no data at all, it is safe for users of any age.

---

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes — for example, if a future version of the app introduces optional features requiring data handling — this document will be updated with a new date and a clear description of what changed.

Future changes will never be retroactive.
